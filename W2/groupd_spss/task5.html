Example

Transform all VQ data from a 7-point scale to a 5-point scale and normalize it to [1 , 4.5].

Dataset: Gaming_Video_Quality_dataset.xlsx

Setup and transfering data format

Simply import the data from the Excel file.

Performing the test

  1. Click "Transform" -> "Compute Variable"
  2. In the “Target Variable” field write “VQ”
  3. In the “Numeric Expression” field write the expression for converting a 7-point scale to a 5-point scale according to the Köster et. Al research paper

    The formula assumes that the 7-point scale spans between 0 and 6. We adapt the formula for a 7-point scale from 1 to 7.
